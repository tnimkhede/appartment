Apartment Management System – Detailed Functional Document (Updated)
1. Introduction

The Apartment Management System organizes all operations of residential complexes, including maintenance, payments, communication, security, facility booking, and incident tracking—ensuring transparency and efficiency.

2. User Roles
2.1 Resident

View and pay bills

Raise tickets

Track visitors & deliveries

Book facilities

View notices & events

2.2 Society Management / Committee

Manage billing & payments

Approve requests

Monitor maintenance

Manage staff & vendors

2.3 Security Staff

Handle visitor entries

Track vehicles

Log deliveries

Respond to emergency alerts

2.4 Maintenance Team

Receive tickets

Update service logs

Manage equipment & repairs
